3D Desktop Effects

Also known as 3D workbench, or 3D effects, this graphical effects are provided by Compiz Fusion package. Compiz Fusion is a set of applications based on the compiz OpenGL compositing manager. Compiz Fusion provides a lot of visial effects. There are desktop cube, flexible windows, burning and many others. System requirements are Nvidia, ATI/AMD, Intel video card with OpenGL support.
